## Market Trends (2024-2025)

### CGC Rising
- Clear #2 position secured
- Global expansion underway
- Improved market acceptance
- CGC Pokemon values climbing
- Gap with PSA resale prices narrowing

### BGS Declining
- Market share drop to 3%
- Customer service missteps
- Slower turnaround
- Still relevant for Black Label collectors
- Unless BGS 10, may not outsell PSA equivalent

### PSA Dominance
- Still "King" of Pokemon grading
- 70%+ market share maintained
- Best resale value consistency
- Infrastructure investments ongoing
- Standards remain consistent despite volume

## Key 2025 Insights
1. **PSA remains gold standard** for resale value
2. **CGC is best budget option** ($12-15 vs $18.99)
3. **CGC Pristine 10 can command premiums** over PSA 10
4. **BGS relevant only for Black Label hunting**
5. **50% of pack-fresh Pokemon cards gem at PSA**
6. **CGC stricter than PSA** on modern cards
7. **Turnaround times increased** across board (demand)
