# Intent & Format Decision
**Keyword**: Pokemon Card Grading Guide: What PSA/BGS Graders Look For in 2025
**Analysis Date**: November 9, 2025

## Search Intent Classification

**Primary Intent**: **Informational + Commercial Investigation**

**User Goals**:
1. Understand what graders look for (centering, corners, edges, surface)
2. Compare grading companies (PSA vs BGS vs CGC)
3. Decide which grading company to use
4. Learn if their cards are worth grading
5. Understand costs, turnaround times, value

**SERP Analysis**:
- Top 10 results are ALL educational guides/blog posts
- No service pages, no direct sales
- Mix of comparison guides + single-topic deep dives
- #1 result is 2021 guide (outdated = opportunity)

## Competitor Content Formats

**Top 3 Formats**:
1. **Long-form educational blog** (3,000-4,000 words) - GGWP Academy #1
2. **How-to guides** - Multiple in top 10
3. **Comparison articles** - PSA vs BGS vs CGC

**Common Elements**:
- What graders look for (4 factors)
- Company-by-company breakdown
- Grading scale explanations
- Before-you-send tips

**Missing Elements** (opportunity):
- 2025 pricing/timing data
- Cost-benefit calculators
- Decision frameworks
- When NOT to grade
- Market trend analysis
- CGC Pristine 10 coverage

## Format Decision: **Comprehensive Educational Guide**

### Chosen Format: Single Pillar Page (5,000-6,000 words)

**Why This Format**:
1. ✅ Matches SERP intent (all top results are guides)
2. ✅ Can include comparison tables (better than competitor)
3. ✅ Allows for FAQ section (PAA coverage)
4. ✅ Can incorporate decision frameworks
5. ✅ Better for 2025 data integration
6. ✅ Easier to maintain/update annually

**Why NOT a Cluster**:
- SERP doesn't show multi-page strategies winning
- User wants ONE comprehensive answer
- Competitors use single-page format successfully
- Easier to rank single authoritative page

### Content Architecture

**Sections** (H2):
1. Introduction: Pokemon Card Grading in 2025
2. What Graders Look For: The 4 Core Factors
   - Centering (with exact PSA measurements)
   - Corners (with visual examples)
   - Edges (with common issues)
   - Surface (most important factor)
3. PSA Grading Standards & Scale (2025 Update)
4. BGS Grading Standards & Scale (2025 Update)
5. CGC Grading Standards & Scale (2025 Update)
6. 2025 Grading Company Comparison
   - Cost comparison table
   - Turnaround time comparison
   - Resale value comparison
   - Market share data
7. Which Grading Company Should You Choose?
   - Decision framework
   - Use case scenarios
8. Is Your Pokemon Card Worth Grading?
   - Cost-benefit analysis
   - When to grade vs when NOT to grade
9. How to Prepare Cards for Grading
10. FAQ Section (15+ questions)

**Supporting Elements**:
- Comparison tables (3-4)
- Decision flowchart
- Cost calculator guidance
- Visual examples
- 2025 statistics
- Expert quotes (PSA/CGC official sources)

## Competitive Advantages

**vs 2021 GGWP Academy Guide**:
1. ✅ **Freshness**: 2025 vs 2021 (4-year gap)
2. ✅ **Data**: Current pricing, times, market share
3. ✅ **Depth**: CGC Pristine 10, gem rates, trends
4. ✅ **UX**: Comparison tables, decision tools
5. ✅ **Comprehensiveness**: When NOT to grade
6. ✅ **Authority**: 2025 statistics, official sources
7. ✅ **FAQ**: PAA coverage (competitor has none)

## Target Metrics

**Content**:
- 5,000-6,000 words (vs 3,155 competitor)
- 15+ FAQ questions
- 5+ comparison tables
- 10+ visual elements

- Images: Alt text optimized

**EEAT**:

## Final Decision

**Format**: **Single comprehensive educational guide (pillar page)**
**Word Count Target**: 5,000-6,000 words
**Primary Differentiator**: **2025-updated data + decision frameworks**
**Secondary Differentiator**: **Comprehensive FAQ + comparison tables**

This format will outrank the 2021 competitor through freshness, depth, and superior UX while matching user intent perfectly.
