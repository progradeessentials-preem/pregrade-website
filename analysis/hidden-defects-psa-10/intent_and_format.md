# Intent & Format Decision

**Keyword:** "10 Hidden Defects That Destroy Your PSA 10 Grade (+ How to Spot Them)"  
**Date:** 2025-11-09  

## Search Intent Analysis

**Primary Intent:** Educational / Informational  
**User Goal:** Learn to identify hidden card defects before submission  
**Stage:** Pre-grading research (consideration phase)  
**Action Desired:** Understand defects → Inspect own cards → Submit to grading (or use PreGrade service)

**User Questions:**
- What defects prevent PSA 10?
- How do I spot hidden defects?
- What should I look for before submitting?
- What tools do I need to inspect cards?
- Can I self-grade accurately?

## SERP Format Analysis

**Competitor Formats:**
1. **OG Cards** - General PSA 10 guide (784 words, basic)
2. **BlockApps** - Science of grading (what makes PSA 10)
3. **Rorz Cards** - Grading scale explained
4. **PSA Official** - Official grading standards
5. **Forums** - Community discussions on defects

**Dominant Format:** Educational blog posts / guides  
**Gap:** No comprehensive defect-specific checklist article  

## Format Decision

✅ **PRIMARY FORMAT: Comprehensive Educational Guide (Single Long-Form Page)**

**Why This Format:**
1. ✅ Matches user intent (how-to, educational)
2. ✅ Competitors use blog/guide format
3. ✅ Allows depth (2000+ words)
4. ✅ Can rank for listicle query ("10 hidden defects")
5. ✅ Supports rich media (images, diagrams)
6. ✅ FAQ-friendly
7. ✅ Schema-rich (Article + HowTo + FAQ)

**NOT a cluster:** Single comprehensive page performs better for:
- Numbered list queries ("10 things")
- Specific how-to queries
- Pre-grading checklist intent

## Content Structure

### Primary Page: `/hidden-defects-psa-10`

**Format:** Educational listicle + how-to guide  
**Target Length:** 2500-3000 words  
**Tone:** Expert, authoritative, practical  

**Sections:**
1. **Hero/Intro** (100 words)
   - Hook: "Your card looks perfect... until PSA finds these"
   - Promise: Learn 10 hidden defects + how to spot them
   
2. **Why Hidden Defects Matter** (200 words)
   - PSA 10 rejection rate
   - Cost of failed submissions
   - PreGrade value proposition
   
3. **10 Hidden Defects** (1500 words - 150 per defect)
   Each defect includes:
   - What it is
   - Why it destroys PSA 10
   - How to spot it
   - Tools needed
   - Visual example (image/diagram)
   
   Defects:
   1. Print Lines (Horizontal/Vertical)
   2. Silvering (Edge whitening)
   3. Holo Scratches (Light vs Heavy)
   4. Soft Corners (vs Hard Corners)
   5. Centering Issues (Front vs Back tolerance)
   6. Roller Marks (Factory defects)
   7. Surface Dents/Indentations
   8. Edge Chipping (Micro vs Macro)
   9. Color Fading/Bleeding
   10. PVC Damage (Storage defects)
   
4. **Inspection Checklist** (300 words)
   - Step-by-step inspection process
   - Tools needed (loupe, black light, angle lighting)
   - Downloadable checklist CTA
   
5. **When to Submit vs When to Hold** (200 words)
   - Grade prediction guide
   - Cost-benefit analysis
   - PreGrade service CTA
   
6. **FAQ Section** (400 words)
   - 8-10 questions covering:
     - Can I fix defects?
     - What lighting is best?
     - Should I use a magnifier?
     - What's the most common hidden defect?
     - How strict is PSA on factory defects?
     - Etc.
   
7. **Conclusion + CTA** (100 words)
   - Recap
   - PreGrade service offer
   - Email capture for checklist
